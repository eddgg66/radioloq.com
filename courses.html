FAQ
Frequently asked questions
What language are the courses taught in? +
Course materials are available in English and German. For the Mastery course, German-language reporting practice is a core component. Q&A sessions can be conducted in English, German, Turkish, Azerbaijani, or Russian.
Are these CME-accredited? +
Currently these courses are not formally CME-accredited. They are continuing education resources developed by a German-trained physician to practical diagnostic standards. Formal accreditation is planned for future iterations.
How long do I have access after purchasing? +
All courses come with lifetime access to all materials, including any future updates to that course module. Community access duration varies by tier — 6 months for Clinical, lifetime for Mastery.
Can I upgrade from a lower to higher tier later? +
Yes. If you purchase the Pilot course and later want to upgrade to Clinical or Mastery, you only pay the difference in price. Contact us to arrange an upgrade.
What does the "letter of recommendation" include? +
Upon completing the Mastery course with demonstrated proficiency (assessed through the mock board exam and case submissions), Dr. Eddy can provide a personalised letter of recommendation for fellowship applications or hospital positions. This is awarded on merit, not automatically.
Is there a refund policy? +
Yes — if you're not satisfied within 14 days of purchase and have completed fewer than 2 modules, we offer a full refund. Contact us at hello@radioloq.com.
