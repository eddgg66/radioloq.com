About me
Adalat Ganjali
Radiology Resident · EvKB Bielefeld, Germany
I work as a physician in Germany's healthcare system with a specialisation in diagnostic radiology. I understand what it feels like to receive a report full of terminology that means nothing — I've seen it countless times. My mission: bringing German diagnostic quality and plain-language clarity across borders and languages.
This service is entirely independent from my role at EvKB Bielefeld hospital and is offered in a personal capacity.

"I designed this service around one question: what would I want for my own family member receiving a scan report they can't understand?"
