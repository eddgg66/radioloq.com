About me
Adalat Ganjali
Radiology Resident · EvKB Bielefeld, Germany
The reason I started Radioloq is simple: quality medical care should be understandable — and accessible — for everyone. With this service, you cut through the bureaucracy and get a clear answer to the question nobody tells you after imaging: "Now what?" I'm a radiology resident in Germany, and I'm here to help you make sense of it — calmly, clearly, in your own language.
This service is entirely independent from my role at EvKB Bielefeld hospital and is offered in a personal capacity.

"Clarity is not a luxury. Understanding your own scan is your right."
